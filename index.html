The Artist

Kriti Chugh

Storyteller Creative Producer Visual Artist Tattoo Creator Filmmaker

Before picking up a tattoo machine, Kriti spent years behind cameras, on sets, and inside stories — crafting narratives that moved people. As a Senior Creative Producer, she learned that the most powerful art comes from the most personal places.

breathingcanvastattoos was born from a simple belief: every person carries stories worth making permanent. Each tattoo begins not with a design, but with a conversation — about memories that shaped you, people who changed you, and moments you never want to forget.

Based in Hyderabad, Kriti transforms her clients' deepest emotions into art they carry forever. This isn't just tattooing — it's collaborative storytelling on skin.

— Kriti

Our Philosophy

Not Just Tattoos.
Living Stories.

We believe every mark on skin should carry meaning. Every line should hold a memory. Every tattoo should breathe with the person who wears it.

✦

Stories Matter

We don't ask "what tattoo do you want?" — we ask "what story do you want to carry with you?" Every piece begins with a deep conversation about meaning, memory and emotion.

◈

Memories Become Art

A grandmother's handwriting. A childhood landscape. The coordinates of where you fell in love. We translate the intangible into permanent visual poetry that lives on your skin.

❋

You Are the Canvas

Your body is not a blank wall to decorate — it's a breathing canvas of lived experiences. We honor that by creating art that feels like it was always meant to be part of you.

"Most tattoo artists sell tattoos. We sell stories translated into permanent art."

Your Tattoo Journey

Getting a tattoo at breathingcanvastattoos is an intentional, collaborative experience — not a transaction.

01

Discover

Share your story, memory or emotion. We listen deeply before we draw.

02

Collaborate

Together we explore ideas, references and visual directions that feel right.

03

Design

Kriti creates custom artwork that translates your narrative into visual poetry.

04

Ink

In a calm, intimate setting, your story becomes permanent art on your skin.

05

We guide you through aftercare so your art heals beautifully, inside and out.

06

Remember
